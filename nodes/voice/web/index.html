    How confident Silero must be to call audio "speech". Lower = more sensitive (catches whispers but also breath/noise).

    Cosine similarity required to confidently match a known speaker. Higher = stricter (fewer false matches, but same speaker may split into multiple profiles).

    Below this similarity, a NEW speaker profile is created. Between this and "match" → assigned as "uncertain" to nearest profile. Raise to encourage new profiles for distinct voices.
